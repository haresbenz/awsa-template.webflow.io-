Why Typography Matters

Typography plays a critical role in how users perceive and interact with content. A well-chosen font improves readability, establishes tone, and supports the overall visual identity of a product.

Using a system-friendly typeface like Inter ensures consistent rendering across platforms and devices.

‍

‍

Key Principles to Keep in Mind

  • Clear hierarchy between headings and body text
  • Comfortable reading width for long paragraphs
  • Balanced spacing between elements
  • Predictable behavior in responsive layouts

These principles help maintain usability without adding unnecessary visual noise.
